Gud Gum Bubble Shooter!

Game Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move the cannon:

  • Left Arrow: Move left
  • Right Arrow: Move right
  • Up Arrow: Shoot

Pop the pink bubbles to get Gud Gum and gain points. Avoid the grey bubbles as they are plastic!

Note: Your score can be redeemed on the Gud Gum website. Click on the scoreboard to visit the site.

This game was created for the final term project in "Strategies for Mobile Marketing" by Group 4 - Comprising Raaj Nishanth, Karthikeyan, Gotham, Dinesh, Arasu, Shloka, and Akanksha.
